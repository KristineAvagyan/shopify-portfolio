FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children? +
Absolutely! We exclusively use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your entire family, including pets and children. All our products are EPA-certified and free from harsh chemicals.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning? +
No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide a key or door code and we handle everything while they're away. All our staff is fully vetted, background-checked, and insured for your peace of mind.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +
Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not 100% happy with any aspect of our service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to make it right — free of charge. If we still can't satisfy you, we offer a full refund.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies? +
Yes! Our team arrives fully equipped with all necessary cleaning supplies and professional-grade equipment. If you prefer we use your own products, just let us know when booking and we'll accommodate your request.
How do I book a recurring cleaning schedule? +
You can book recurring cleanings (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) directly through our booking form, by phone, or by email. Recurring clients receive a 15% discount on all services and priority scheduling.
What areas do you serve? +
We currently serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs within a 30-mile radius. Contact us with your zip code and we'll confirm availability in your area.
